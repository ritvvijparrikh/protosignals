  • Summary: From Checkpoint, we realized that any solution to misinformation needs to operate at scale. This is especially true for a country like India with its religious, linguistic, and cultural diversity. Hence, we started ^^investigating misinformation from the vantage point of cognitive psychology, which operates at a more primal level of the brain.^^ The study found six factors that make misinformation more persuasive on WhatsApp.
