PROTOGraph CMS

  • Topic:: The Business of News 🗞️
  • Authors:: Nasr ul Hadi Ritvvij Parrikh
  • Format:: Software Prototype Webinar
  • Patrons:: International Center for Journalists, ICFJ Knight Fellowship
  • Status:: Developed
  • Publishing Status:: Folded and open sourced due to lack of funding
  • URL:: https://github.com/heyProto/ProtoGraph.CMS
  • Summary: We first attempted to understand and contribute towards improving the business of news way back in 2017. As technologists, our first instinct was to develop software focussed on discovering new product models for news. Hence, from 2017 till late 2018, ICFJ commissioned pykih.com to build a modular, serverless, structured-content CMS for data-driven journalism projects.
  • Outcome: Newsrooms in India like TheWire, Newslaundry, Jagran, IndiaSpend, and think tanks like Oxfam and CEEW piloted the CMS.
  • Learning: Over time, we realized that while these tools were valuable in making newsrooms more efficient, they were still only solving a peripheral issue concerning the business of news. Consequently, the product was folded and open-sourced on GitHub.
