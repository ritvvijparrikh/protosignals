  • Summary: We launched a ^^verification tip line on WhatsApp for the Indian General Elections of 2019^^. Citizens were encouraged to forward rumors that they receive to our tip line number. The goal of the project was to design a workflow that would increase the throughput of verification teams. The second goal was to study what kind of misinformation floats around inside WhatsApp.

  • Outcome: In 45 days, citizens forwarded ^^80,000 unique rumors^^ to the tip line. The workflow we designed enabled our team of 8 analysts to verify 514 pieces of misinformation.

  • Learning: A significant realization was that the volume of misinformation out there is an order of magnitude more than verification and fact-checking efforts.
